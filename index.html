The Kurdish Nation

Kurds are an unprotected minority of Indo-European descent, and are indigenous people of Mesopotamia, encompassed by Zagros and Taurus mountain ranges

Kurds are united through race, culture and language, and while religious diversity has existed among Kurds for centuries, Sunni Isalm is the most adhered religion

Kurdistan’s flag is the most important symbol of Kurdish identity, it’s main charachterstic is the golden sun emblem with 21 beams representing Nawroz
